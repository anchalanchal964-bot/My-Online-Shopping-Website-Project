Types of Websites

Explore different categories of websites with examples

Blog Websites

A blog is an online journal or informational website where individuals or groups share content on specific topics. Blogs often allow readers to comment and engage.

Examples: WordPress blogs, travel blogs, personal diaries.

E-Commerce Websites

E-commerce websites allow businesses or individuals to sell products and services online. These include product listings, shopping carts, and payment systems.

Examples: Amazon, Flipkart, eBay.

Portfolio Websites

A portfolio website showcases an individual’s or organization’s work. Commonly used by designers, developers, artists, and photographers.

Examples: Graphic designer portfolio, photography portfolio.

News & Media Websites

News websites publish current events, articles, and updates worldwide. These websites are regularly updated to provide fresh content.

Examples: BBC News, CNN, The Times of India.

Educational Websites

Educational websites provide learning resources, tutorials, and online courses. These sites can be used by schools, universities, and e-learning platforms.

Examples: Coursera, Khan Academy, edX.
