Website Hosting URL Registration

How URLs are Registered

A domain name (like example.com) is the human-readable address of your website. To make your site accessible, you need to register a domain name.

Steps in Domain Registration

  1. Choose a domain name (e.g., mywebsite.com).
  2. Check availability through a domain registrar.
  3. Register the domain by paying an annual fee.
  4. Connect the domain with your hosting provider using DNS records.
